Why PayRam?

Traditional payment infrastructure
has three problems.

Problem 01

Getting started takes too long

Most PSPs require lengthy applications, document reviews, and approval queues. You wait weeks before accepting your first payment.

PayRam Advantage

No approval process. Deploy in under 10 minutes.

Problem 02

You do not own your funds

Stripe, PayPal, and centralized crypto processors hold your money until they decide to release it. Rolling reserves. T+2 settlements. Funds frozen without warning.

PayRam Advantage

Non-custodial by design. Funds settle to your wallet, not ours.

Problem 03

Your infrastructure can be taken away

A policy change, a chargeback spike, or an account flag can shut down your ability to accept payments overnight. High-volume and cross-border merchants are especially exposed.

PayRam Advantage

Self-hosted and censorship-resistant. No one can pull the plug.
